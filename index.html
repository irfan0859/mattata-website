What is mattata?

mattata is a plugin-based bot for Telegram Messenger, written in Lua and open source. You are free to fork mattata and, well, do what you wish with it - just abide by the license.

Originally based upon otouto, mattata has been re-shaped and improved over time to provide an immersive experience for users of any age. Notable features include a Cleverbot implementation (which uses my own Lua library), group administration functionality and a robust configurable plugin system. These plugins all provide a unique purpose and are frequently improved.
